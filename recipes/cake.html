How to make Cake

a sweet, baked, breadlike food, made with or without shortening, and usually containing flour, sugar, baking powder or soda, eggs, and liquid flavoring. 2. a flat, thin mass of bread, esp. unleavened bread.

Cake is often served as a celebratory dish on ceremonial occasions, such as weddings, anniversaries, and birthdays.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • salt
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • butter

Steps

  1. Get a clean bowl
  2. Add all the ingredients in the bowl
  3. Mix all the ingredients
  4. Now place the mixture in the oven
  5. Once its done, take it out and let it cool
