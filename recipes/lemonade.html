How to make Lemonade

Lemonade is a colourless sweet fizzy drink. A drink that is made from lemons, sugar, and water and is not fizzy can also be referred to as lemonade.

Bright sour acidity is essential to make lemonade refreshing. Sweetness: A good lemonade should have just enough sweetness to balance the sour lemon flavor.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Soda
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Pour water in a bowl
  2. Squeeze lemon into it
  3. Add sugar and soda and mix well
  4. Add ice cubes
  5. Serve
